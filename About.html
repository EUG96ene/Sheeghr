Why us?

We conduct all business with the highest standards

We firmly believe in the power of collaboration. We have established strong partnerships with industries, businesses, and individuals. By providing an efficient and reliable platform for buying and selling scrap metal, we connect suppliers and customers, facilitating the seamless flow of materials. Together, we contribute to a greener way of doing business.
Through educational initiatives and outreach programs, we inspire individuals and businesses alike to embrace sustainability. Together, we can make a positive impact on our environment.

Sustainability lies at the core of our operations. By diverting scrap metal from landfills and reintroducing it into the manufacturing cycle, we significantly reduce the need for extracting raw materials. This, in turn, decreases energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions. Our commitment to sustainable practices sets us apart and positions us as a responsible corporate citizen

Our Vision

Our purpose is to improve lives through what we do.

We understand the importance of recycling in conserving natural resources and reducing waste. By promoting the circular economy, we aim to minimize the extraction of raw materials and reduce the carbon footprint associated with traditional manufacturing processes.

“To provide sustainable solutions for managing scrap metal, contributing to a greener and cleaner future."

For Sheeghr Invested our vision is to revolutionize the scrap metal industry by becoming a leading force in innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
